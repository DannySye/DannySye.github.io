About Eiraka Ryaitu

Eiraka Ryaitu (Our Voice) is a multimedia platform owned by Eryaitu Media (U) Limited, located in Bushenyi-Ishaka Municipality, Bushenyi District, Southwestern Uganda.

We document and tell stories of sustainable living from people in communities of Greater Bushenyi and Southwestern Uganda. Our areas of interest are GOVERNANCE, PUBLIC HEALTH, and ENVIRONMENT. We also mobilize local communities to participate in conversations about responsible living and sustainable development. We do this through organising weekly Eiraka Fireplace Conversations and podcasts that are shared in different formats for consumption by communities, including duty bearers.

Fireplace Conversations

Dialogue in Action
Community Insights
Storytelling for Change

The mind of this space is to promote dialogue and encourage critical thinking and storytelling as drivers of social change in communities.

These conversations are interactive in nature, involving a moderator and guests/panellists. The audience has a chance to gain insights through the guest’s experience and thoughts on the subject.

This informal setting is designed to make everyone feel relaxed and yet leave with more information than they came in with. The topics discussed at Fireplace Conversations calm the feelings of the participants and lift their spirits as they slide into the weekend.

This way, participants benefit from sharing ideas, knowledge, and skills, and this helps society members have their leadership, sense of responsibility, and survival skill set boosted in different ways.

These interactions often come in question-and-answer formats, triggering the audience to think about the questions being asked to the guest as they (audience) also prepare to actively be part.

After hearing the panelists or guests break new ground within topics of the day, participants also share and express themselves on the topic before panelists are invited to give their concluding remarks as the night show is wound down.

The topics often discussed are drawn from areas of public health, environment, and governance, and each topic is tailored to provide solution(s) to a certain challenge.

Governance

Leadership in Dialogue
Community Governance
Policy Solutions

Governance at Eiraka Ryaitu focuses on fostering inclusive decision-making through Fireplace Conversations, where local leaders and community members discuss policies impacting Greater Bushenyi.

These sessions encourage critical thinking about leadership roles, featuring moderators and guests who share insights on effective governance, inspiring participants to contribute solutions.

The relaxed environment promotes open dialogue, helping attendees leave with enhanced understanding of governance challenges and strategies for sustainable development.

Through interactive Q&A, participants explore questions on leadership and responsibility, boosting their skills to address community governance issues.

After guest discussions, the audience shares perspectives, leading to actionable insights as panelists conclude with tailored solutions for governance challenges.

Topics are drawn from real community needs, aiming to strengthen leadership and accountability in Southwestern Uganda.
