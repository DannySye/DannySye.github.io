Fireplace Conversations

Dialogue in Action
Community Insights
Storytelling for Change

Welcome to Fireplace Conversations, a platform where community voices ignite change. This space fosters dialogue, critical thinking, and storytelling to address challenges in Greater Bushenyi and Southwestern Uganda.

Hosted weekly, these sessions feature moderators, guests, and an interactive audience, delving into topics like public health, governance, and environment. Each conversation aims to inspire solutions and empower participants.

The mind of this space is to promote dialogue and encourage critical thinking and storytelling as drivers of social change in communities.

These conversations are interactive in nature, involving a moderator and guests/panellists. The audience has a chance to gain insights through the guest’s experience and thoughts on the subject.

This informal setting is designed to make everyone feel relaxed and yet leave with more information than they came in with. The topics discussed at Fireplace Conversations calm the feelings of the participants and lift their spirits as they slide into the weekend.

This way, participants benefit from sharing ideas, knowledge, and skills, and this helps society members have their leadership, sense of responsibility, and survival skill set boosted in different ways.

These interactions often come in question-and-answer formats, triggering the audience to think about the questions being asked to the guest as they (audience) also prepare to actively be part.

After hearing the panelists or guests break new ground within topics of the day, participants also share and express themselves on the topic before panelists are invited to give their concluding remarks as the night show is wound down.

The topics often discussed are drawn from areas of public health, environment, and governance, and each topic is tailored to provide solution(s) to a certain challenge.
